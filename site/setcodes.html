Expansion code symbols
Prime Set. These are the original CMC cards.
Clash of the Webcomics. These are cards from CMC's first expansion, "Clash of the Webcomics" which contains webcomic-related cards.
Unknown Expeditions. These are cards from the 2nd expansion, a mini-expansion of only Location cards.
Principalities. These are cards from CMC's second real expansion, Principalities, which contains cards continuing the storyline about the Cult of Melrak.
Voucher Redemptions. These cards are cards available only through Voucher Redemption codes.
Presents. These are cards from the CMC Secret Service and other special events.
Implementer Cards. These cards are not publically available- Administration only.
Prizes. These are donation cards.
